# ShieldNet v2.0 — Moderation Operations Center

## Purpose

The v2.0 operations layer consolidates all member cases into one guild-wide moderation queue.

## Features

- Guild-wide case queue with pagination.
- Search by case title or Discord member name.
- Filters by status, priority, assignee and overdue SLA.
- Priorities: low, normal, high and urgent.
- Case deadline and automatic overdue calculation.
- First-response timestamp when a case enters investigating state.
- Operational counters for open, investigating, overdue, urgent, unassigned, due today and resolved during the last seven days.
- Moderator workload overview.
- Direct editing of status, priority, owner and deadline.
- Quick navigation from the guild dashboard.
