# ShieldNet v1.9 — Evidence & Appeals Center

Adds evidence records and appeals to Member Cases.

## Evidence
- Types: link, screenshot, message, document, other.
- Title, source URL and administrative notes.
- Evidence is isolated by guild and member case.
- Authorized administrators can delete incorrect records.

## Appeals
- Register an appeal for an existing case.
- Statuses: submitted, under review, accepted, rejected, withdrawn.
- Store the statement, submitter label and final decision.
- Reviewer and review timestamp are recorded automatically when an appeal is finalized.

## Database migration
`0021_evidence` revises `0020_cases`.
